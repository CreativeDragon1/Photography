About

Aditya Koushik

Aditya Koushik is a passionate photographer capturing landscapes, moments, and unique perspectives. Through his lens, ordinary scenes become powerful visual stories.

Based in India, Aditya travels the country seeking the extraordinary in the everyday — from the misty peaks of the Western Ghats to the vibrant streets of old cities.
